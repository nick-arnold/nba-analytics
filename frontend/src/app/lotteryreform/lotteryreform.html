Lottery Reform Simulator

What would NBA draft history look like under a fairer lottery?

2004–2025 22 Draft Classes
This simulator re-runs the NBA draft lottery from any starting year using three proposed reforms: flat odds (every non-playoff team has an equal shot at any pick), a consecutive block (teams can't land top-2 in back-to-back years), and a frequency cap (no team can land a top pick more than twice in a four-year window). Teams blocked by these rules enter a second lottery competing for picks 5–14. Configure the rules, pick a start year, and run the simulation.
